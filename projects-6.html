This is an assignment that I did while a senior in high school. I was to emulate the common box-model style that all websites use to organize their information. The first part, margin, is the part that spaces the outside-most area of of the box. The second part, border, is either the invisible or visible wall that seperates the inside of the page from the outside. The third part, padding, is the spacing around the content part of the page. Last part is of course where the content goes. The assignment was overall simple and fairly easy to complete. I like how close the model looks to how it looks on dev tools.
